Casa Magazines

What attracts readers to certain magazines?

By: Giulia Tacchi

Txt Img Multi

Founded in the 1970s, Casa Magazines has been selling magazines for over half a decade. Since the early 2000s, Ali is at the head of the store, and to him the success of this small magazine shop in the West Village lies in its selection and in its loyal community.

“One of the reasons why I keep coming back is the excellent selection [of magazines]” says Louis, who has been coming to Casa Magazines for around 18 years. Its success lies in the people who keep believing in it, and support it.
